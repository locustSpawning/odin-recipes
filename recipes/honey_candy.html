Homemade Honey Candy

A simple sweet candy for spring ~

Feel free to add lemon, mint, ginger, or other spices! P.S. these can be dropped in tea!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • silicone candy mold

Steps:

  1. Pour sugar into a pot
  2. Add water
  3. Add honey
  4. Combine together
  5. Bring mixture to a boil
  6. Let bubbles settle
  7. Pour into mold and let sit over night
